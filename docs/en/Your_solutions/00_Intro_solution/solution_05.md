# Vector Resolution: Calculating Components

### Definitions
- **Magnitude ($|\vec{A}|$)**: The total length of the vector.
- **Horizontal Component ($A_x$)**: The projection of the vector on the x-axis.
- **Vertical Component ($A_y$)**: The projection of the vector on the y-axis.

### Formulas

$$
A_x = |\vec{A}| \cos(\theta)
$$

$$
A_y = |\vec{A}| \sin(\theta)
$$

---

### Step-by-Step Calculation

**1. Identify the given values:**
- $|\vec{A}| = 15$
- $\theta = 60^\circ$

**2. Solve for the horizontal component ($A_x$):**

$$
A_x = 15 \cdot \cos(60^\circ)
$$

$$
A_x = 15 \cdot 0.5 = 7.5
$$

**3. Solve for the vertical component ($A_y$):**

$$
A_y = 15 \cdot \sin(60^\circ)
$$

$$
A_y = 15 \cdot \frac{\sqrt{3}}{2} \approx 12.99
$$

### Summary
The vector $\vec{A}$ can be written in component form as:

$$
\vec{A} = (7.5, 12.99)
$$
