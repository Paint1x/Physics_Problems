# 8. EM Spectrum

**The Simple Explanation:**
Electromagnetic radiation is categorized by how "wide" the waves are. High energy waves (like Gamma) are tightly packed and tiny. Low energy waves (like Radio) are very long and wide.

**The Order (Shortest to Longest Wavelength):**
1. **Gamma rays** (Tiny, size of an atomic nucleus)
2. **X-rays** (Size of atoms)
3. **Ultraviolet** (Size of molecules)
4. **Infrared** (Size of a pinpoint)
5. **Microwaves** (Size of a bumblebee)
6. **Radio waves** (Massive, size of buildings or mountains)
