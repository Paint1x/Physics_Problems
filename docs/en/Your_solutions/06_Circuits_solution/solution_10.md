# 10. Average Current

**The Simple Explanation:**
We don't need calculus here because we are just looking for the average over a chunk of time. We just divide the total amount of electricity by the total amount of time. 

**The Setup:**
* Total Charge ($Q$) = $30 \text{ C}$
* Total Time ($t$) = $2 \text{ ms} = 0.002 \text{ s}$

**Step-by-Step Math:**

$$
I_{avg} = \frac{Q}{t}
$$

$$
I_{avg} = \frac{30}{0.002}
$$

$$
I_{avg} = 15,000 \text{ A}
$$
